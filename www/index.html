About The Mirror

"This app can structure your reflection and make consistency easier — but the actual rewiring happens in the 2 minutes you spend being honest with yourself in it, not in the code. The radar chart, the named future self, the urge timer — these are scaffolding to make that honesty easier to sustain for months instead of days. The app is the mirror. You are still the one who has to look into it."
Identity Categories
The 6 traits being trained
Taqwa — Consciousness of Allah
Discipline — Acting regardless of feeling
Self-control — Delay, restraint, silence
Gratitude — Humility, appreciation
Honesty — Self-accountability, no excuses
Brotherhood — Relationships, generosity, character
The Core Principle
How the app works
Every single action in the app is framed as a vote for an identity, not a task to complete. The radar chart shows your identity shape evolving over time. This is not gamification — every number maps to a real identity category.
Self-Distancing
Why the third-person framing
All reflective prompts use second/third person framing (e.g., "What did [Name] avoid today?") instead of first-person. This is deliberate — self-distancing reduces emotional reactivity and improves honest self-assessment.
